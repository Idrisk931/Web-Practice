THE MIGHTY LEGIONS

The Roman Legions were a formidable force in their prime and no army could match their impegranable shield wall and lethal gladii.

At the battle of Alesia under Divius Julius Guis Ceaser, a heavily outnumbered roman army not only managed to defeat but utterly crushed a confederation of gallic tribes under vercingetorix.

No other army could march 30 miles and still construct a heavily fortified marching camp at the end of the day. Only the iron roman discipline made this possible.
